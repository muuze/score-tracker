Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new game?
Tap the + button in the top right corner of the main screen.
How do I delete a game?
Swipe left on any game in the list and tap Delete.
What is the difference between Basic and Rounds scoring?
  • Basic — freely add or subtract points from each player at any time.
  • Rounds — enter scores round by round and see a full round history table.
How do I end a game?
Open the game and tap End Game at the bottom of the screen.
How do I start a new game with the same players?
After a game ends, tap New Game at the bottom of the screen. All players carry over with scores reset to zero.
What is Score Tracker Pro?
A one-time purchase that unlocks Rounds scoring mode, unlimited games, and removes the ad banner.
How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
Open the upgrade sheet and tap Restore Purchase.
How many games can I have without Pro?
Up to 3 games at a time. You can delete a game to make room for a new one, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited games.
