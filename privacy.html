Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

Score Tracker is a free iOS app for tracking scores in games. All game data (games, players, and scores) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data We Collect

Score Tracker itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Device identifiers (such as IDFA, if you grant tracking permission)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction and usage data
  • Diagnostic data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.

App Tracking Transparency

On first launch, the app will ask for your permission to track your device for advertising purposes. If you decline, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized. You can change this at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

In-App Purchases

Score Tracker offers a one-time Pro upgrade purchase. All purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
